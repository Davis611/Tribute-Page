The life of John Clem

The story of John Clem aka:The Drummer Boy

  • In October 1863 just two years after John Clem joined the 22nd michigan.
    John Clem was captured in Georgia by the Confederate cavalrymen while detailed as a train guard.
  • John Clem served as a drummer boy at the battle of shiloh.
    He came very close to loosing his life when a fragment from shrapnel shell went through his drum.Knocking him unconscious. His comrades found and rescued him.
    The battle of shiloh never happened on paper the 22nd Michigan was never constituted at the time of the battle.
  • In the battle of Chickamauga John Clem rode artillery caisson to the front and was wielding a musket that was trimmed to fit his size.
